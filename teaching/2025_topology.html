Topology (KSM1C03)
Syllabus

Topological Spaces and Continuous Functions, Connectedness and compactness, Tychonoff's theorem, Countability and Separation Axioms, normal and regular spaces, Urysohn-Tietze theorems, Complete Metric Spaces and Function Spaces, Baire Category Theorem.

Resources
  • Topology by James Munkres
  • General Topology by Stephen Willard
  • Elementary Topology Problem Book by O. Ya. Viro, O. A. Ivanov, N. Yu. Netsvetaev, & V. M. Kharlamov
  • Counterexamples in Topology by Lynn A. Steen & J. Arthur Seebach, Jr.
  • Algebraic Topology by Tammo tom Dieck
